### Automated, intelligent cryptocurrency tax audit and reconciliation.

Cryptobasis is a software-as-a-service platform for cryptocurrency tax audit and reconciliation. We help individual taxpayers, small businesses, and tax professionals identify errors and inconsistencies in crypto tax positions — the kinds of problems that emerge when transactions are spread across multiple exchanges, wallets, and chains, and reported figures don't quite tie out.

The platform ingests transaction history, reconstructs cost basis and lot accounting, and surfaces specific discrepancies with the underlying evidence — so users can correct returns with confidence rather than guesswork.
