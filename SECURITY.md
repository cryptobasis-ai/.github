# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you believe you've found a security issue in Cryptobasis software or infrastructure, please report it privately. Do not file a public GitHub issue.

**Email:** [security@cryptobasis.ai](mailto:security@cryptobasis.ai)

Please include:

- A description of the issue and the potential impact
- Steps to reproduce, or a proof of concept
- Any relevant logs, screenshots, or supporting material
- Your name and contact information (or a pseudonym, if you prefer to remain anonymous)

## Our commitment

- We will acknowledge receipt of your report within **3 business days**.
- We will provide an initial assessment within **10 business days**.
- We will keep you informed of progress as we investigate and remediate.
- We will credit you in the disclosure (if you wish) once a fix is published, unless you prefer to remain anonymous.

## Scope

In scope:
- Cryptobasis web application and APIs at `cryptobasis.ai` and subdomains
- Public software released by Cryptobasis Technologies LLC

Out of scope:
- Third-party services we integrate with (please report those to the respective vendor)
- Issues that require physical access to a user's device or account credentials
- Social engineering of Cryptobasis staff or contractors
- Denial-of-service attacks, volumetric attacks, or rate-limit testing

## Safe harbor

We will not pursue or support legal action against researchers who:

- Make a good-faith effort to comply with this policy
- Avoid privacy violations, data destruction, and service disruption
- Give us a reasonable time to investigate and remediate before any public disclosure
- Do not exfiltrate, retain, or share user data beyond what is strictly necessary to demonstrate the issue

If in doubt about whether something is in scope or whether your testing is permitted, email us first and ask.
